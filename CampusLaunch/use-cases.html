Student Business Use Cases

From selling lecture notes to launching mini courses. Discover how university students are turning their skills and knowledge into real income with CampusLaunch.

University students are sitting on a goldmine of knowledge, skills, and creative talent and most don't realise it. CampusLaunch makes it easy to turn what you already know into a profitable digital product or service business. Thousands of students across more than 120 universities are already earning through the platform.

The most successful student businesses on CampusLaunch share a few things in common: they solve a real problem for other students, they're built on knowledge the seller already has, and they use our AI-powered tools to handle the business side from product descriptions to payment processing.

Below, you'll find the most popular categories of student businesses and detailed examples of what's working right now. If any of these ideas resonate, learn how to get started in three simple steps.

Popular Categories

What Students Are Selling

These are the top-performing business categories on CampusLaunch, based on real student revenue data.

Sell Academic Notes

Monetise your lecture notes and revision guides. Upload once and earn repeatedly as students in your course and future cohorts purchase your materials. Some top sellers earn over £500/month from notes alone.

Top Seller

Tutoring Services

Create professional booking pages, set your own hours and rates, and accept secure payments for online or in-person tutoring. CampusLaunch handles scheduling and payment so you can focus on teaching.

High Demand

Digital Templates

Design and sell resume templates, social media kits, presentation decks, and spreadsheet tools. Students and professionals buy these repeatedly making templates one of the best passive income streams.

Passive Income

Mini Courses

Package your expertise into bite-sized courses on programming, essay writing, data analysis, or marketing. Mini courses let you charge premium prices for structured learning content.

Scalable

Creative Assets

Sell photography presets, video editing templates, Canva designs, music beats, or illustration packs. Creative students are earning from digital assets that take a few hours to create but sell for months.

Trending

Freelance Services

Offer data analysis, web development, copywriting, or graphic design services through your CampusLaunch storefront. Set up service packages with clear deliverables and pricing.

High Value

Success in Action

The student tutoring business is one of the fastest-growing categories on CampusLaunch. Students offering programming tutoring, essay support, and maths help are consistently hitting £800–£1,500/month, working just 10–15 hours per week around their own studies.

Digital products are equally compelling because they scale without extra work. A well-made set of revision notes or a professional resume template can sell hundreds of copies with zero additional effort after the initial creation. That's the power of digital - build once, earn repeatedly.

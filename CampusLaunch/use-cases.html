Student Business Use Cases

From selling lecture notes to launching mini courses. Discover how university students are turning their skills and knowledge into real income with CampusLaunch.

CampusLaunch makes it easy to turn what you know into a fully designed, AI-generated online store no technical setup, no blank templates, no starting from scratch. You describe your business idea, AI builds your store, and you launch. Thousands of students across more than 120 universities are already selling through CampusLaunch-powered stores.

The most successful student stores on CampusLaunch all start the same way: with a simple prompt. Whether it's "a tutoring booking site for GCSE students" or "a digital template shop for business students," AI generates the complete store branding, layout, product pages, and content in seconds. Then the student customises, adds their products, and launches.

Below are the most popular store categories on CampusLaunch. If any of these match your idea, learn how to generate your store in three steps.

Success in Action

The student tutoring business is one of the fastest-growing categories on CampusLaunch. Students offering programming tutoring, essay support, and maths help are consistently hitting £800–£1,500/month, working just 10–15 hours per week around their own studies.

Digital products are equally compelling because they scale without extra work. A well-made set of revision notes or a professional resume template can sell hundreds of copies with zero additional effort after the initial creation. That's the power of digital - build once, earn repeatedly.

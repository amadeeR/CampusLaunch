How It Works

From Idea to Live Store in Three Steps

CampusLaunch works differently from traditional website builders. You do not build your shop, AI does. Just describe your business, let AI generate everything, then customise and launch. The whole process takes minutes.

1

Describe Your Business Idea

Type a short prompt describing your business, for example: "a handmade jewellery for university students" or "a digital revision notes shop for law students." That is all the AI needs to get started. No forms to fill in. No settings to configure.

2

AI Generates Your Complete Store

In seconds, AI builds your entire online store. That includes the homepage layout, product sections, navigation, branding style, visual assets, and written content, all tailored to your specific business idea. No templates to choose. No pages to assemble manually.

3

Customise, Add Payments & Launch

Review your AI-generated store, then use the drag-and-drop editor to personalise it. Add products, update text, change colours, rearrange sections. Connect a secure payment provider in a few clicks and go live. Your shop is ready to take orders.

Why We are Different

Traditional website builders like Wix, Squarespace, or Shopify give you blank templates and expect you to fill everything in yourself. According to Google Trends, interest in online business tools and AI-powered entrepreneurship has increased significantly in recent years. You have to choose a layout, write all the text, source images, set up pages, and configure your site from scratch. That can take days, even weeks.

We work the opposite way. You describe your business in one sentence and AI generates the entire store for you: structure, layout, branding, product sections, images, and content. The site is built before you have clicked a single button in an editor.

AI Does the Building. You Do the Launching.

Once your AI-generated shop is ready, the drag-and-drop editor lets you take over and make it your own. Swap images, adjust the copy, add products, change colours, or leave it exactly as AI built it. Either way, you are launching a professional-looking shop in minutes, not weeks.

Connecting payments is just as simple. Add a secure payment provider through a built-in plugin or integration, and your site is ready to take real orders. No developer needed. No complicated merchant accounts to set up.

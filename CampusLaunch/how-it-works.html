How to Start a Business as a University Student

From idea to income in under 15 minutes. CampusLaunch's student side hustle platform makes launching your first online business as simple as three steps.

1

Generate Your Business Idea

Use our AI-powered idea generator to discover profitable student business opportunities matched to your skills, interests, and academic field. The system analyses trending markets and suggests ideas with genuine earning potential.

2

Build Your Online Store

Create a professional, mobile-ready digital storefront in minutes using our drag-and-drop store builder. Upload products, set prices, and customise your branding without writing a single line of code.

3

Launch & Start Earning

Go live and start accepting secure payments from customers. Track revenue, collect emails, and grow your business using CampusLaunch's built-in analytics tools.

Why CampusLaunch is Different

Starting an online business traditionally requires technical skills, startup capital, and months of setup. CampusLaunch eliminates all of those barriers. Our student side hustle platform is specifically designed for university students who want to earn money without sacrificing their academic performance.

The process begins with our AI idea generator, which doesn't just throw random suggestions at you. It considers trending markets, your skill set, and what's realistically achievable alongside a full course load. Students have used these ideas to launch everything from tutoring businesses to digital template shops.

From Idea to First Sale

Once you have your idea, the store builder gets you online fast. The average CampusLaunch store takes under 15 minutes to set up, and every template is designed for conversion. Meaning your products look professional from day one.

Payment processing is built in and secure, so you don't need to worry about merchant accounts or complicated integrations. Customers can pay with cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay, and funds land in your bank account within days.

Our analytics dashboard helps you understand what's working and what isn't, tracking views, conversions, and revenue in real-time. This means you can optimise your store and products based on actual data, not guesswork.

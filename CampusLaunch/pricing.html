FAQ

Common Questions

How does the AI store builder work?

You type a short description of your business, and AI generates your complete online store. Homepage, product sections, branding, content, and images are all created automatically. You can then customise anything with the drag-and-drop editor.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely. Switch between plans whenever you want. If you downgrade from Pro, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a student discount?

Our pricing is already designed for student budgets. At $9/month (or $7.50 with annual billing), Pro is among the most affordable business platforms available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely with bank-grade encryption.

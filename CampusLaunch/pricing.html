Why Students Love Our Pricing

We designed CampusLaunch to be the most affordable business platform for students. Our Free plan isn't a limited trial. It's a fully functional store builder that lets you list products, accept payments, and track analytics. Many students run successful businesses entirely on the Free plan.

The Pro plan is built for students who want to scale. For less than the cost of two coffees per month, you get access to AI-powered business tools that professional sellers pay hundreds for including AI-generated product descriptions, advanced analytics, custom domain support, and unlimited product listings.

Choosing the annual plan saves you 17%, bringing the monthly cost down to just $7.50. That investment typically pays for itself within the first week of increased sales, thanks to AI-optimised product copy and advanced insights.

Not sure which plan is right for you? Start with Free, explore our full feature list, and upgrade when you're ready. There's no commitment and no credit card required to begin.

FAQ

Common Questions

Can I really start for free?

Yes. The Free plan includes a working store, payment processing, and basic analytics. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no expiry date.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely. Switch between plans whenever you want. If you downgrade from Pro, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a student discount?

Our pricing is already designed for student budgets. At $9/month (or $7.50 with annual billing), Pro is among the most affordable business platforms available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely with bank-grade encryption.

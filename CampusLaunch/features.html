AI Builds Your Store. You Launch It.

CampusLaunch is not a traditional website builder. You describe your business in one prompt and AI generates everything: store structure, branding, product pages, images, and written content. Then you customise and launch.

A Store Builder That Builds for You

CampusLaunch is a new kind of online store builder, one where AI does the building for you. Research from Statista shows that global ecommerce continues to grow rapidly, creating new opportunities for students to launch digital businesses.

The result includes a designed homepage, product sections, branding style, store layout, and written content, all tailored to your specific business. It is the fastest way to go from idea to professional store, and it is built specifically for students and beginners who want results without the technical complexity.

Once your AI-generated store is ready, you are in full control. Use the built-in drag-and-drop editor to rearrange sections, update text, add products, change colours, or refine the design. AI generates the foundation and you make it yours.

All Features

Everything Included, Powered by AI

Six powerful capabilities that make CampusLaunch the fastest and easiest way to launch an online store, no experience needed.

01

AI Store Builder

Enter one prompt, like "a handmade jewellery store for university students", and AI automatically generates your entire online store. Homepage, product sections, navigation, layout, and branding are all created in seconds.

02

AI Image Generation

No professional product photos? CampusLaunch automatically generates high-quality images for your store: product visuals, hero banners, and promotional graphics tailored to your brand.

03

AI Content Generation

AI writes your product descriptions, store headlines, marketing copy, and section text automatically. Every word is crafted to sound professional and convert visitors into buyers.

04

Drag-and-Drop Customisation

Once AI generates your store, you have full control. Use the intuitive editor to rearrange sections, edit any text, add new products, change colours, and adjust the layout to match your vision.

05

Simple Secure Payments

Connect trusted payment providers to your store in just a few clicks. Your customers can pay with cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. You get paid directly and securely.

06

Student-First Platform

CampusLaunch is built for students who want to start an online business without technical skills. Affordable, fast, and simple enough to set up between lectures.

AI Generates. You Customise. Then Launch.

CampusLaunch flips the traditional store-building model. Instead of starting with a blank canvas and building everything manually, AI generates your complete store first. From the moment you submit your prompt, you will have a fully structured, designed, and written online store ready to review.

Your homepage layout is set, your product sections are populated, your brand colours and typography are chosen, and your store copy is written, all before you have touched a single setting. Think of it as having a professional web designer and copywriter build your store in seconds.

From there, the drag-and-drop editor lets you take over. Swap images, edit headlines, reorder sections, update pricing, add new products, or change the colour scheme. The AI does the hard part so you only spend time on the creative decisions you actually enjoy.

AI Student Business Tools

Powerful, easy-to-use tools designed to help university students launch, manage, and scale profitable online businesses without any technical experience.

CampusLaunch brings together everything a student entrepreneur needs in one intuitive platform. Whether you're looking to sell digital products as a student, launch a tutoring service, or monetise your lecture notes, our AI-powered student ecommerce platform removes the technical barriers so you can focus on creating and earning.

Unlike generic website builders, CampusLaunch is purpose-built for the student lifestyle. We understand that you're balancing coursework, exams, and a social life so every tool is designed to save you time and get results fast. Our AI does the heavy lifting: from generating business ideas to writing product descriptions that convert.

With built-in analytics, secure payment processing, and an audience-building email capture system, CampusLaunch gives you the same tools professional sellers use at a price that works for student budgets.

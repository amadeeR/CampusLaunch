Platform Features

AI Builds Your Store. You Launch It.

CampusLaunch is not a traditional website builder. Instead of manually building your store piece by piece, you describe your business in one prompt and AI generates everything store structure, branding, product pages, images, and written content. Then you customise with ease.

CampusLaunch is a new kind of online store builder one where AI does the building for you. Rather than picking templates, dragging sections around, and writing copy from scratch, you simply describe your business idea in a short prompt. The AI takes that description and generates a complete, ready-to-launch online store.

The result includes a designed homepage, product sections, branding style, store layout, and written content all tailored to your specific business. It's the fastest way to go from idea to professional store, and it's built specifically for students, beginners, and small entrepreneurs who want results without the technical complexity.

Once your AI-generated store is ready, you're in full control. Use the built-in drag and drop editor to rearrange sections, update text, add products, change colours, or refine the design. AI generates the foundation you make it yours.

All Features

Everything Included Powered by AI

Six powerful capabilities that make CampusLaunch the fastest and easiest way to launch an online store no experience needed.

AI Store Builder

Enter one prompt like "a handmade jewellery store for university students" and AI automatically generates your entire online store. Homepage, product sections, navigation, store layout, and branding style are all created in seconds. No templates to configure. No pages to build manually. AI does the work so you can launch fast.

AI Image Generation

Don't have professional product photos or banner graphics? CampusLaunch automatically generates high-quality images for your store product visuals, hero banners, and promotional graphics tailored to your brand. Launch a polished, visual store even if you have no images of your own.

AI Content Generation

AI writes your product descriptions, store headlines, marketing copy, and section text automatically. Every word is crafted to sound professional and convert visitors into buyers. No marketing background required just review, tweak if you like, and publish.

Drag-and-Drop Customisation

Once AI generates your store, you have full control. Use the intuitive drag-and-drop editor to rearrange sections, edit any text, add new products, change colours, and adjust the layout to match your vision. AI builds the foundation you personalise every detail.

Simple Secure Payments

Connect trusted payment providers to your store in just a few clicks. No complicated merchant setup, no coding required. Your customers can pay with cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. You get paid directly and securely so you can focus on selling, not admin.

Student-Friendly Platform

CampusLaunch is built for students, beginners, and small entrepreneurs who want to start an online business without technical skills. Everything from the interface to the pricing is designed around your life affordable, fast, and simple enough to set up between lectures.

AI Generates. You Customise. Then Launch.

CampusLaunch flips the traditional store-building model. Instead of starting with a blank canvas and building everything manually, AI generates your complete store first. From the moment you submit your prompt, you'll have a fully structured, designed, and written online store ready to review.

That means your homepage layout is set, your product sections are populated, your brand colours and typography are chosen, and your store copy is written all before you've touched a single setting. Think of it as having a professional web designer and copywriter build your store in seconds.

From there, the drag-and-drop editor lets you take over. Swap images, edit headlines, reorder sections, update pricing, add new products, or change the colour scheme. The AI does the hard part so you only spend time on the creative decisions you actually enjoy.
